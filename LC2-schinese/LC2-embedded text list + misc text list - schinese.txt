CUTSCENE 1

1781  Outskirts of Rome
Five years after his great victory against the evil Von Viltheim Family, Efrain Lecarde, the Knight of The Church, was summoned by the Pope. 
The most troubling events had been reported somewhere in France. 
Efrain was about to enter the worst nightmare imaginable… 

1781年 罗马的郊外
在战胜了邪恶的冯.维尔特海姆家族五年以后，艾尔弗林.李卡德，作为教会直属的骑士，被教皇召见了。
报告中说在法国的各地，发生了危害人类的灵异事件。
艾尔弗林需要尽快查明真相，但是他进入了他所能想象的最糟糕的的噩梦当中...
